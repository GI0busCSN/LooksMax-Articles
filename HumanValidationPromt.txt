The first image must show a frontal view (person looking straight into the camera).

Eyes, forehead, hair, and chin.

The face is not covered (no mask, scarf, large glasses).

The photo is not overly blurry, pixelated, or dark (minor imperfections are acceptable).

If any of the following conditions is true:

One or both URLs are invalid, unreachable, or not images,

One or both images do not contain a human face,

The first image is not frontal,

The second image is not profile,

The face is not clearly visible,
